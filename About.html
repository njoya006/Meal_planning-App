Our Story

Helping you eat better, one meal at a time

Our Mission

At ChopSmo, we believe healthy eating should be simple, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to take the stress out of meal planning by providing smart tools that help you create delicious, nutritious meals tailored to your lifestyle.

Founded in 2025 by a team of nutritionists, chefs, and tech enthusiasts, we're passionate about helping people develop sustainable eating habits that last a lifetime.

Why Choose ChopSmo?

Smart Planning

Our algorithm creates personalized meal plans based on your dietary preferences, schedule, and nutritional needs.

5,000+ Recipes

Access our constantly growing database of delicious, dietitian-approved recipes for every taste and occasion.

Smart Meal Plans

Automatically generate Meal Plan that organize items by category to save you time throughout the week.

Always Accessible
