Account & Setup

You can sign up using your email or social media accounts. Go to "Sign Up" and follow the prompts. We only require your email and a password to get started.

You can browse recipes without an account, but you'll need to sign up to save meal plans, access grocery lists, and use premium features.

Meal Planning

Our smart algorithm suggests meals based on your dietary preferences, available cooking time, and ingredients you already have. You can customize any suggestion with one click.

Dietary Needs

Yes! Set your dietary restrictions in Profile > Dietary Preferences to automatically filter recipes. We track 12 common allergens including nuts, dairy, and gluten.

Grocery Lists

Yes! Any saved meal plan automatically creates an organized grocery list sorted by category (produce, dairy, etc.). Lists sync across all your devices in real-time.
